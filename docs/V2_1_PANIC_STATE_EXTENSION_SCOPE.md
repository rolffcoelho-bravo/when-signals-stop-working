# Version 2.1 Panic-State Diagnostic Extension

## Approved role

The panic-state module is approved as a secondary diagnostic extension after completion of the frozen Version 2 confirmatory programme. It is not part of the D3 locked evaluation and cannot alter any Version 2 confirmatory decision.

## Non-interference controls

The extension must not modify:

- the RSI `NO_PIPELINE_ADMITTED` decision;
- the D2C Bollinger pipeline or its canonical hash;
- D3 predictions or evaluation boundaries;
- the confirmatory statistical or economic gates;
- final Version 2 multiplicity-adjusted verdicts.

## Initial terminology

The initial output must use `PANIC_CONSISTENT_REGIME`, not a causal claim of investor panic. Stronger attribution requires richer liquidation, leverage, liquidity, order-book, derivatives, and cross-venue evidence.

## Candidate diagnostic inputs

- downside-return acceleration and drawdown velocity;
- volatility jumps and downside semivariance;
- abnormal volume and dependence spikes;
- funding, open-interest, and liquidation stress where available;
- bid-ask spread expansion and order-book depth collapse;
- stablecoin, exchange-flow, and cross-venue dislocation measures.

The extension will be implemented under a separate V2.1 protocol, namespace, lock, tests, and evidence report after the Version 2 confirmatory verdict is frozen.
