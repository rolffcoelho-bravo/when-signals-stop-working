# Figure Catalog

The framework generates vector-first SVG figures for GitHub, publications, and high-resolution export. SVG is the default to reduce memory pressure and preserve typography and line quality.

1. **Market and signal anatomy** - SOL price with Bollinger Bands, RSI, %B, and filtered regime probabilities.
2. **Validation evidence dashboard** - fold-level predictive gain, fold-level economic edge, confidence intervals, and regime-conditioned performance.
3. **Probability calibration** - benchmark and candidate probability reliability by signal family.
4. **Online monitoring panels** - cumulative net evidence, rolling incremental edge, and structural-change CUSUM for each signal.
5. **Regime evidence matrix** - predictive and economic contribution across range, trend, and stress states.

Every figure contains a compact source and method footer. The generated report ends with a full references and reproducibility frame.
