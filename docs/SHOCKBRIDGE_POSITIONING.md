# ShockBridge Pulse Positioning

Do not position this as another RSI or Bollinger backtest.

The stronger positioning is:

> ShockBridge Pulse develops benchmark-relative methods for determining whether market signals contain incremental information, under which structural regimes they remain valid, and when their recent contribution has deteriorated enough to require suspension.

The methodological value comes from combining:

- conventional signal interpretation;
- non-indicator benchmarks;
- chronological validation;
- leakage controls;
- economic costs;
- regime dependence;
- confidence intervals;
- explicit failure governance.

The repository demonstrates a reusable process. RSI and Bollinger Bands are two adapters inside that process, not the intellectual center of the work.
