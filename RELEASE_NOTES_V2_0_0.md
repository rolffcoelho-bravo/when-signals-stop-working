## Determination

Version 2 tested whether expanded horizons, alternative technical-signal interpretations, restrained nonlinear models, estimation windows, filtered-state conditioning, calibration, and selective abstention could establish stable incremental value under a frozen nested-validation and methodology-locked evaluation design.

The final primary-case determination is:

| Signal family | Final status |
|---|---|
| RSI | `NO_PIPELINE_ADMITTED` |
| Bollinger Bands | `NO_INCREMENTAL_EVIDENCE` |

The frozen Bollinger pipeline produced favourable average benchmark-relative predictive and economic contributions in parts of the locked sample. Those contributions did not satisfy the complete multiplicity-adjusted, dependence-aware predictive and economic confidence requirements. Stable incremental value was therefore not established.
